## Details

Tatar cultural and educational society "DUSLYK"

The cultural and educational center is located in Abkhazia, in the town of Gagra. The center holds events and cozy gatherings and celebrates national holidays accompanied by traditional Tatar songs. The center works actively to teach the traditions and customs of the Tatar people.

It holds themed evenings dedicated to famous Tatars. Director — Saniya Mingalimova.

Location: Gagra, Abazgaa St. 59/1-71, Abkhazia. Contacts: 591 171, Duslik
