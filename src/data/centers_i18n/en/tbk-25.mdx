## Details

Tatar-Bashkir cultural center "Duslyk" in Petropavlovsk

The North Kazakhstan Oblast Tatar-Bashkir public center "Duslyk" in Petropavlovsk is dedicated to preserving and promoting Tatar-Bashkir culture.

The center holds cultural events, charity drives, and celebrations aimed at strengthening interethnic harmony.
