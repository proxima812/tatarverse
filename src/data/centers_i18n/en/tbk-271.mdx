## Details

The organization "Tatars in Belgium" is one of the most renowned and active Tatar communities in Europe. The Tatar union holds master classes on national cuisine and organizes days of Tatar culture at children's camps. The union "Tatars in Belgium" cooperates with the Russian Center of Science and organizes poetry evenings featuring Tatar poets. Director — Liliya Valeeva.
