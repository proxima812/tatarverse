## Details

Tatar society "Miras", Austria

The Tatar community "Miras" of Austria was founded in 2013. The organization's goal is to preserve traditions and unite people who care about Tatar culture. The organization regularly holds gatherings of Tatars and organizes the Sabantuy festival. The ensembles of the "Miras" society are regular participants in celebrations, and its musical groups, singers, and dancers delight the residents of Austria with traditional Tatar songs and dances.
