# At the founding assembly held on 24 November 2023, by the unanimous decision of five local Tatar national-cultural autonomies of the Lomonosovsky, Sosnovoborsky, Vsevolozhsky, Vyborgsky, and Priozersky districts of Leningrad Oblast, the Regional Public Organization "Tatar National-Cultural Autonomy of Leningrad Oblast" (ROO TNKA LO) was established, and Airat Akdasovich Mannanov was elected chairman of the organization. The full name of the organization according to its charter: REGIONAL PUBLIC ORGANIZATION "TATAR NATIONAL-CULTURAL AUTONOMY OF LENINGRAD OBLAST"
